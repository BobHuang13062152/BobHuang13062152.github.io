About Us

The Web Intelligence and Data Mining Laboratory (WIDM Lab) at National Central University is dedicated to researching and developing intelligent systems to address complex real-world problems.

Our research spans Web information extraction, machine learning, data mining, conversational agents, and knowledge graphs, with applications in web intelligence, information integration, legal AI, human-computer interaction, and educational systems.

Research Interests

Agentic AI & Automation

Agentic AI, GUI Automation

Legal AI

Legal Judgement Predication, Collision Care Guide chatbot, Legigation Visualization

Web Intelligence

Mobile Web Creator, Opinion mining for social study, Meetup Event Extraction, Point-of-Interest Extraction

AI for Education

EduACT, Storybot

Smart Campus

NCUFree, WiFiPass, Mobile Advertising

Information Extraction

Web Information Extraction and Text Mining, Web data extraction (IEPAD, FiVaTech, Data API Creator)

Data Mining

Periodic pattern mining
