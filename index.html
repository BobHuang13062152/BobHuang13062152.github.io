About Us

The Web Intelligence and Data Mining Laboratory (WIDM Lab) at National Central University is dedicated to researching and developing intelligent systems to address complex real-world problems. Our research spans Web information extraction, machine learning, data mining, conversational agents, and knowledge graphs.
